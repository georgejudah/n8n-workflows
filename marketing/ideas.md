# Marketing Automation - Ideas

## Workflows Built

1. **Daily Motivational Quotes Email Campaign** - Scheduled automation (7 AM daily) that generates AI-powered motivational quotes using GPT-4o-mini, fetches subscriber list from Google Sheets, and sends personalized emails via Gmail to engage and nurture your audience

2. **LinkedIn Post Generator** - Form-based workflow that collects post topic and target audience, uses AI agents with Tavily web search to research current trends, generates professional LinkedIn post content with GPT-4o, creates custom marketing graphics with DALL-E based on AI-generated image prompts, and automatically publishes to LinkedIn with confirmation email - complete end-to-end social media content creation
